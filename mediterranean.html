My Top 3 Mediterranean Restaurants in NYC

The first place on my list is 12 Chairs Cafe !This is one of my go-to spots, located in downtown NYC. I recommend the warm pita which is freshly made daily and trying all the different dipping spreads. 12 Chairs Cafe is a great place to go for a casual dinner with excellent drinks and even better food. The atmosphere is vibrant and fun and the indoor and outdoor seating gives off a nice ambience. It most definitely will not dissapoint! Image from: "12 Chairs Cafe"

My next favorite is Lola Taverna . Lola Taverna is a modern style restaurant with beautiful indoor and outdoor seating. The outdoor seating is covered in flowers and makes you almost feel like youâ€™re outside of NYC. It is perfect for a date night and located in SoHo. The pricing of the food is reasonable but the drinks are a little overpriced. However, they are delicious! I reccomend the trying the lamb chops and Greek salad! Image from: "Secret NYC"

Another amazing spot is Shuka . Shuka is a vibrant and modern restaurant that is great for a date night or bigger group party! The first time I went I wandered in spontaneously and was pleasantly surprised by how delicious my meal was. I recommend making a reservation before hand because we had to sit at the bar and it was pretty crowded. I recommend dipping their fresh pita into their whipped feta dip - it is truly amazing! Shuka also has great options for vegetarians and vegans! Image from: "Shuka"
